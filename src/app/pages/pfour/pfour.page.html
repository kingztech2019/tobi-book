Language of pride

Babylon takes her place in the hearts of men, a very popular and striking influence, manipulating all flesh as ruled by her. Her delicacies are ever pleasant to the lustful tendencies of mankind. After his fall, he devised means with his skills to get his desired goal against divinity. The form of this knowledge comes in the academics of this world you live in, where man ascends in knowledge and sees himself as the maker of himself without giving honour to whom its due. This is the faulty notion of the conceiver, Satan, who in his arrogance, exalts himself with his gifts and drags others with such self-deception. Here comes the quadripartite order of the Babylonian appearance of your enemy in their peaceful ventures.

Satan

Satan, the principal enemy of man was created perfect but allowed pride to enslave him to a spiritual wreck. Satan sealed his fate for a second death and hopes to take as many with him. He never forces you to sin but suggests logics to break your wall of truth.

The world is the seat of Satan’s territory fashioned to steer man’s way against his creator, she hopes to gather as many armies of men to wage war against everything that stands for light. The world my friend is evil because it has been placed under the god of sin and pride who tickles fleshly lusts, just as it had been from the garden of Eden, in the time of Adam and Eve. God wants us to be enemy of the world’s standards and system which is far from liberating the soul of man. The existence of this standard is to prove the way of peace that cannot be duplicated because its only one. The way of the world is empty and suicidal, souls languish under her reins, Satan entrenches the system and use it to enslave mortal men lost in the lust of the eyes, the lust of the flesh and the pride of life. We must be an enemy of this heinous system and raise the banner of the truth always. Darkness looms over the world, gross darkness but with the truth comes the light that renders the light bearer the head of all captors, releasing all the captives in darkness into a marvelous light of redemption, a price paid by the truth and light himself, Jesus Christ.

The world’s end is ticking as time bomb and in no time will explode. Seek the truth and be freed from the attractions of this world. The world you live in is transient.
